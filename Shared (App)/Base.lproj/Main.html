To enable No Shorts: Blocker for YouTube on your iPhone or iPad:
1. Open the Settings app.
2. Scroll down and select Safari.
3. Tap Extensions.
4. Find No Shorts: Blocker for YouTube and enable it.

For more help, see Apple's guide to Safari extensions on iPhone and iPad.

To enable No Shorts: Blocker for YouTube on your Mac:
1. Open Safari.
2. In the menu bar, go to Safari > Settings (or Preferences on older versions).
3. Select the Extensions tab.
4. Check the box next to No Shorts: Blocker for YouTube to enable it.

For full instructions, visit Apple's guide to Safari extensions on Mac.
